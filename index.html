    Creative Standard

    Five Rules Behind This Build

    01 Archive Integrity

    Every visual direction must map to a verifiable historical source, not trend imitation.

    02 Intentional Excess

    Richness is allowed only when hierarchy remains clear and reading order stays effortless.

    03 System, Not Decoration

    Components are reusable, parameterized, and behavior-driven from controls to rendering.

    04 Responsive Rhythm

    Mobile collapses preserve drama through scale, spacing, and contrast without breaking usability.

    05 Motion With Purpose

    Animation acts as pacing and orientation, not ambient distraction.
