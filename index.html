Method

From Archive Research To Interface Decisions

Research First

Layout and tone are based on documented posters and museum records, not generic "retro Japan" moodboards.

Extract Visual DNA

We mapped recurring traits: disciplined grids, high-contrast color, symbolic forms, and provocative collage strategies.

Translate To UX

Navigation, card rhythm, component hierarchy, and motion all use those references while staying usable on mobile and desktop.

Artist Study

1960s-1970s Japanese Design Works Reviewed

Each card links a historical work to a concrete UI move used in this site.

1961-1963

Yusaku Kamekura

Tokyo 1964 official posters and photogravure execution.

Interface impact: strict alignment, forceful empty space, and strong emblematic framing in hero and navigation.

1960-1975

Ikko Tanaka

Early anti-violence posters and postwar modern-tradition fusion.

Interface impact: geometric typographic rhythm and restrained visual hierarchy with cultural formality.

1965+

Tadanori Yokoo

Persona-era poster language with rising-sun and collage shocks.

Interface impact: controlled bursts of dense color, symbolic badges, and bold narrative sectioning.

1970-1973

Kiyoshi Awazu

Metabolism-linked posters and anti-functionalist pop geometry.

Interface impact: layered shapes, cross-disciplinary texture, and expressive graphic surfaces in module blocks.

1970s

Eiko Ishioka

Parco campaigns that challenged norms and visual stereotypes.

Interface impact: assertive copy tone, high-contrast callouts, and editorial framing that favors stance over decoration.

1960s scene

Network Effect

Ginza creative circles and Persona-driven illustrator culture.

Interface impact: modular yet mixed visual language, balancing modernist order with expressive cultural variety.

UX Notes

How Historical Style Was Kept Usable

  • Reference, Then Reduction

    Complex poster motifs were reduced to reusable interface grammar.

  • Motion As Tempo

    Staggered reveal timing echoes print-sequence pacing, not gimmicks.

  • Legibility Priority

    The grid compresses cleanly so historical references never damage readability on small screens.
