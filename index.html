    UX Refactor Rules

    What Changed In UI, UX, And Background

    01 Illustration-First Layout

    Interfaces now read like artboards: layered blocks, vivid color zones, and deliberate visual storytelling.

    02 Hand-Drawn Texture System

    Grain overlays, rough borders, and doodle motifs replace sterile gradients and flat geometry.

    03 Functional Creative Controls

    Every control impacts generated output directly: palette, density, structure, motion, and re-seeding.

    04 Strong Readability

    Despite expressive visuals, hierarchy is explicit and interactions stay predictable on desktop and mobile.

    05 Background As Experience

    The background is now an active design layer with symbolic doodles, depth cues, and rhythm across sections.
